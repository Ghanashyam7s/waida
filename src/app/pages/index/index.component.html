About us

Western Australian Indian Dental (WAID) Association Inc is a not-for-profit association of dentists of Indian descent & dental graduates with similar interests. It was established in 2017 and has been registered in 2020. WAID stands to provide a platform for dentists of Indian origin to meet at regular intervals for social gatherings and professional learning.
